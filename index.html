How it shipped

Course production was slow and manual—every lesson hand-formatted, every QA pass tracked in scattered docs.
Action

Result
40+ courses shipped end-to-end with faster production cycles and near-zero repetitive formatting work.

How it shipped
Problem
Learners were quietly dropping off mid-course and nobody knew exactly where—or why.
Action
Instrumented completion funnels in BigQuery and Looker Studio, found the exact modules where attention died, and restructured them.
Result
Lifted course completion rates—and turned dashboards into a standing habit, not a one-off audit.
